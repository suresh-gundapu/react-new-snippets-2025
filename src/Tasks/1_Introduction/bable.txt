### ðŸ”§ Why React needs Babel?

* Modern syntax (like `let`, `const`, arrow functions, async/await)

Babel:

* Converts JSX to `React.createElement(...)`
* Transpiles ES6+ to ES5

## âœ… How Babel Works in React App?
